Rules & Obligations


Rules governing the system of competitions and players, which are the basis for organizing competitions and determine the objectives and the right way to play

General Rules


It is forbidden to change the team to any player before two seasons of playing with current club

When the player changes the current team, the player is not allowed to select one of the front teams if he is late on seventh place

The player is allowed to change the team after one season if the substitute team is one of the teams after the seventh place

If the player wants to choose one of the front teams after two seasons on the last switch, then he automatically gets the club in seventh place

The duration of Matches in any competitions is not more than 9 minutes

The third-placed team in UEFA Champions League groups stage, does not qualify for UEFA Europe League

If one is canceled, UEFA Champions League or UEFA Europa League, and a team wins the other championship, then it is the draw between the top 4 teams in third place in the group stage and the fifth and sixth place in the league, to play vs one of them in UEFA Super Cup

Specified Rules


Premier League: A League, total of 38 matches, is the main determinant of the season, 12 matches difficulty is "Super Star" and 26 "Top Player"

Emirates FA Cup: A Knouck-out Tournament Starts with Round of 32, A round of it is played every 7 PL matches

FA Communitiy Sheild / UEFA Super Cup: A One Match Tournament Starts Before the beginning of the season, which (FA C.S.) is played first

UEFA Champions League: It consists of - group stage - Then -Knouck-out stage -, Qualified for teams is 1st,2nd,3rd,4th places
Timing of play :: Round 1,2,3,4,5,6 (at rounds 3,6,9,12,15,18) // Round of 16 (at rounds 21,23) // Round of 8 (at rounds 26,28) // Round of 4 (at rounds 31,33) // Final (at round 37)

UEFA Europa League: It consists of - group stage - Then -Knouck-out stage -, Qualified for teams is 5th,6th,7th places And FA Cup Winner or 8th Place
Timing of play :: Round 1,2,3,4,5,6 (at rounds 3,6,9,12,15,18) // Round of 32 (at rounds 19,20) // Round of 16 (at rounds 21,23) // Round of 8 (at rounds 26,28) // Round of 4 (at rounds 31,33) // Final (at round 37)

Africa Cup of Nations: A Knouck-out Tournament Starts with Round of 14, Played every 2 Seasons, Starts in half season of PL

World Cup Qualifications:A Group Tournament Starts with 3 Groups, Played every 3 Seasons, Qualifying teams are the top three teams and The two teams with the best second place continue to the Supplement.
Timing of play :: Round 1 (at round 7) // Round 2 (at round 12) // Round 3 (at round 15) // Round 4 (at round 20) // Round 5 (at round 24) // Round 6 (at round 28)
Supplement :: Round 1 (at round 30) // Round 2 (at round 31) // Round 3 (at round 32) // Round 4 (at round 33)

World Cup of Nations: A group then Knouck-out Tournament Starts with Round of 32, Played every 3 Seasons, Starts after the end of PL
